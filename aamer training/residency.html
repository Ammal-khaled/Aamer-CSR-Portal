Residency Services

Normal Residency: Spouse and children only. Parents not included.

Golden Residency: Spouse, children, and parents included. Nomination must be applied via Aamer app before applying.

Processing Time: 2–5 working days

Fees: Vary per residency type. Confirm via app.

  • Ask for Application ID / Job ID
  • Verify residency type requested
  • Inform about Golden vs Normal eligibility
  • End call with standard closing
“Normal residency includes spouse and children only. Golden residency includes spouse, children, and parents. 
Please apply through the Aamer app for Golden Visa nomination first.”
