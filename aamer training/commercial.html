Commercial Licenses-Full Details

What is a Freelance License?

Allows an individual to work independently without a company or sponsor, provide professional services, and legally issue contracts in their own name.

Licensing Authority

TAMM platform under Abu Dhabi Department of Economic Development issues the Freelancer Permit, allowing self-sponsored residence visa.

Permitted Professions

Design, digital marketing, translation, training, consulting, photography, programming, editing, media production, journalism, and other listed professions.

Required Documents
  • Valid passport
  • Recent passport-sized photo
  • CV / Resume
  • Educational or professional certificates (if any)
  • Tenancy contract or proof of residence in Abu Dhabi
  • Completed Freelancer Permit application on TAMM
Costs

Freelancer Permit: approx. AED 1,200 – 1,500 per year.
Residence visa issuance: approx. AED 3,000 – 5,000 (depending on insurance and medical test)

Residence Visa

Self-sponsored residence visa possible after obtaining Freelancer Permit. Requirements: valid passport, health insurance, tenancy contract, medical fitness. Duration: 1 or 2 years, renewable.

Family Sponsorship

Allowed for spouse and children if minimum monthly income AED 4,000 (or 3,000 with accommodation), residence valid at least 6 months, tenancy contract, and health insurance.

Benefits
  • Work independently without a company or sponsor
  • Legally contract and open bank account in your name
  • Possibility to obtain a residence visa and sponsor family
  • Flexibility to provide services to multiple clients or companies
