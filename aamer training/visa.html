International & Tourism Visa

Description: Multiple-entry UAE tourist visas, Saudi tourism visas. All applications submitted via the Aamer app.

Fees: Varies depending on visa type. Check app for accurate fees.

Visa Services – Full Details

Description

Multi-entry tourist visa valid for 5 years. Each stay allowed up to 90 days, can be extended once. Maximum continuous stay: 180 days.

Other Documents
  • Children under 18 are considered accompanying the applicant.
  • Applicants over 18 must submit the application themselves with required documents.
  • Extension allowed for the same 90-day period.
Service Duration

Up to 1 working day after receiving all documents.

Service Fees

AED 4,950.00 (incl. 5% VAT)

Description

Tourist visa to Saudi Arabia for leisure and tourism. Single or multiple-entry options depending on request.

Other Documents
  • Passport valid for at least 6 months
  • Recent passport photo
  • Application submitted via Aamer app
Service Duration

1–2 working days depending on documentation.

Service Fees

Check Aamer app for exact fees (varies by type).
