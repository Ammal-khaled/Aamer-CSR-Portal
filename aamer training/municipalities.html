Municipalities & Transport

Overview

This section covers the Municipalities & Transport services that Aamer provides support for — including Thawtheeq (Tenancy Contracts) and Unit Valuation Certificates. These services are part of the official municipality systems used across the UAE.

Quick note: In Thawtheeq, the lessor can issue the contract directly in the system. If the tenant initiates the request, the lessor must approve it inside the system before issuance.

Thawtheeq (Tenancy Contract)

Service Description

Thawtheeq registers tenancy contracts in the municipality system. Lessor can issue directly; tenant requests require lessor approval in the system.

Service Policies

  • Lessor approval is mandatory for tenant requests.
  • Documents must be valid and clear.
  • Commercial-use properties may require additional approvals.

Service Duration

1 business day after receiving documents and payment.

Service Fees

105 AED (incl. 5% VAT)

Required Documents

  • Passport & Emirates ID copies.
  • Signed tenancy contract.
  • Ownership proof (title deed).

Unit Valuation Certificate

Service Description

The valuation certificate provides the official value of a property unit, often used for Golden Visa, mortgage assessments, and transfer purposes.

Service Policies

  • Ownership documents must be valid.
  • Off-plan units require developer confirmation.
  • Mortgage or disputes may delay issuance.

Service Duration

1–2 business days

Service Fees

1,500 AED (incl. 5% VAT)

Required Documents

  • Title deed or ownership proof.
  • Passport & Emirates ID.
  • Sales agreement or developer letter (for off-plan).

CSR Guidance & Scripts

  1. Confirm the service type (Thawtheeq / Valuation).
  2. Verify and collect required documents.
  3. Confirm payment and record receipt.
  4. Submit through Aamer Hub.
  5. If lessor approval required → follow up.
  6. Track municipality updates.
  7. Send the customer final copy / certificate.
  8. Close ticket with proper notes.
