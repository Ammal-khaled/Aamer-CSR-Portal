Judicial & Legal Services

Overview

Description: Full suite of Judicial & Notary services — traffic transfers, attestations, POA/notary, legal notices, digital marriage contracts, family disputes, claims registration, and corporate contract attestations. Each service includes details below (requirements, duration, fees, notes).

Common notes: Most services conducted via video conference (Cisco Webex). Aamer will contact applicant for UAE PASS and payment links. Non-Arabic documents must be translated (105 AED unless stated otherwise).

Important: All Judicial Services must first be applied for through the relevant court before requesting assistance from us. Applications must also be submitted through the Aamer app. The CSR’s role is strictly advisory: they should guide the customer to complete the court application and use the app, and then contact us afterward for further support. CSRs do not have direct access to Judicial Services—they only hand the request to the responsible PR or department for processing.

CSR Guidance & Scripts

  1. Greet customer: “How can I help you today?”
  2. Ask service name (POA, marriage contract, claim) — do not promise timelines.
  3. Verify identity & payment. Agents must provide POA.
  4. Check documents; request translation/authentication if missing (105 AED).
  5. Inform if Webex/UAE PASS required; Aamer will contact to schedule.
  6. Explain typical SLA 1–2 business days; judicial review may extend timeline.
  7. Log request; escalate to specialist if unclear or complex.
Short Opening Script
“Hello, this is [Your Name] from Aamer. How can I assist you with judicial services today?”
Judicial-specific Script
“Which service do you need: POA/notary, family dispute, contract attestation, traffic transfer? If agent, confirm valid POA. I will check payment & documents and schedule Webex; Aamer will contact for UAE PASS. Typical processing 1–2 business days; final judicial timelines may vary.”
Escalation & Notes
  • Provide checklist if documents missing; ask for re-upload or support submission.
  • Inform translation/authentication fee (105 AED); foreign documents must be legalized.
  • Do not promise court outcomes or timelines — only processing steps.
Trainer note: Verify whether to route query to Judicial, Attestation, or Notary. If unsure, escalate to specialist.
