Purpose

The UAE Job Seeker Visa (Job Exploration Entry Permit) allows individuals whose work visas have been canceled or expired to stay legally in the UAE to search for a new job without leaving the country.

Grace Period After Visa Cancellation

After canceling a work visa, individuals usually receive a grace period of 30 to 180 days, depending on the visa type and situation. During this time, they can look for a new job or adjust their visa status. Confirm exact duration with ICP or GDRFA (if in Dubai).

Options if Grace Period Ends Without Employment

Option A ‚Äì Apply for Job Seeker Visa:

  • Duration: 60, 90, or 120 days
  • Purpose: Legally stay in the UAE to search for work
  • Requirements:
    • Bachelor‚Äôs degree or equivalent (attested)
    • Proof of financial solvency
    • Valid passport (minimum 6 months)
    • In some cases, a refundable deposit
  • Application: Online via ICP (https://icp.gov.ae) or authorized typing centers

Option B ‚Äì Exit and Re-enter on a Visit/Tourist Visa: They can leave the UAE and re-enter on a visit or tourist visa to continue job search, but the Job Seeker Visa is preferred for legal clarity.

Once a Job is Found

After receiving a job offer, the new employer will process the employment and residence visa, updating the legal status accordingly.
